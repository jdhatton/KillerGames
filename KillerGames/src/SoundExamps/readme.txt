There are four directories here:

  McDonalds/
    - basic Applet play() and AudioClip examples
    - used in chapter 7

  SoundPlayer/
    -lots of small examples; mostly Java Sound
    - used in chapters 7 and 9

  LoadersTests/
    - using the ClipsLoader and MidisLoader classes
    - used in chapter 8

  SynthSound/
    - sampled audio and sequence synthesis
    - used in chapter 10


Visit each one for details of how to compile their code.
